# Unit Economics & Business Model

## Value Pricing Model

TokenLeak does not compete on standard SaaS per-seat pricing. As a financial intelligence tool, pricing is aligned with value creation (capital recovered).

### 1. Free "Radar" Audit
- **Cost**: $0
- **Value Delivery**: Identifies the presence of leaks, displays the Leak Radar, and reveals the total "Recovered Runway" metric.
- **Goal**: Virality, email capture, and establishing trust.

### 2. Pro "Execution" Tier
- **Cost**: 10% of identified annual savings, or a flat $299/audit.
- **Value Delivery**: Unlocks the exact Tool Recommendations, Confidence Explanations, and actionable execution plans to downgrade or consolidate tools.

### 3. Enterprise "Continuous" Monitoring
- **Cost**: $499/month
- **Value Delivery**: Integration with Ramp/Brex APIs to continuously monitor spend. Alerts fractional CFOs when duplicate tools are purchased or seats remain idle for >30 days.

## LTV / CAC Assumptions
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: Expected to be extremely low (<$50) initially due to organic sharing of the Leak Radar and VC partner distribution.
- **LTV (Life Time Value)**: 
  - For one-off audits: $299.
  - For continuous monitoring: $5,988/yr.
- **Margin**: 95%+ software margin. The core intelligence engine is entirely computationally inexpensive and requires no human-in-the-loop processing.
